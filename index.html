The Centers of Excellence

These centers may change or grow to fit the needs of different sectors of government.

Cloud Adoption

Help agencies adopt modern practices and policies, integrating the best of digital technologies and management methods into their organization operations and structure.

Contact Center

Support agencies in connecting customers with the right resources and expertise across all information channels.

Customer Experience

Work with agencies to develop a better understanding of their customers and their needs and help translate those findings into actions that create outstanding experiences.

Data Analytics

Provide data and analytics best practices, instrument metrics to support service delivery processes, and builds tools that facilitate continuous process improvement.

Infrastructure Optimization

Assist agencies with the development of a unified and optimized IT infrastructure, leveraging federally owned data centers, cloud computing services, and commercial data centers.
