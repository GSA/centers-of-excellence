Infrastructure Optimization

Assists in optimizing data centers and reducing infrastructure costs.

Service Offerings | Service Catalog | Latest Updates

The Infrastructure Optimization CoE supports agencies in: consolidating federated data centers into a single enterprise facility, modernizing the application landscape, driving out IT infrastructure cost inefficiencies, improving security, and positioning the agency for future infrastructure improvements.

Service Offerings

  • Architecture assessments
  • Optimization planning
  • Network consolidation
  • Data center consolidation
  • New business models e.g. co-location
