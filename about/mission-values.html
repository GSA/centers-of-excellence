Objectives and Solution Competencies

The GSA Centers of Excellence (CoE) were established to accelerate IT modernization across government to:

  • Improve the public experience,
  • Increase operational efficiency, and
  • Reduce legacy IT spending across the government.

To accomplish these objectives, the CoEs centralize top government tech talent, leverage private-sector best practices, and operate with a teaming mindset to collaborate across government departments and agencies.The CoE has teams focused on five functional areas:

  • Cloud Adoption
  • Contact Center
  • Customer Experience
  • Data Analytics
  • Infrastructure Optimization
