About the Innovation Adoption Community of Practice

Our purpose is to build innovation and intrapreneurship capacity across the federal government by generating and sharing useful information, advice, and assistance.

We invite members to:

  • Connect with new, practicing, and expert intrapreneurs in government.
  • Learn to leverage available tools and technologies, including shared solutions.
  • Explore your approaches to challenges, failure, how we work, and what’s possible.
  • Collaborate with each other to create real change by sharing questions, stories, and advice.

Events

The IA CoP regularly hosts opportunities to:

  • connect with peers, trail blazers and thought leaders,
  • learn how to use relevant frameworks and honing your skills for making good things happen exploring common challenges and ways to turn them into opportunities,
  • and collaborate to create a shared resource or solution.

Event updates and registration are accessible through the IA CoP listserv.

Join Us!

Any federal employee is welcome to become a member of the IA CoP. Through the IA CoP listserv, members receive information about how to:

  • Join Monthly community meetings
  • Attend virtual trainings & events
  • Connect with their peers through the CoP’s listserv

To join, email INNOVATION-ADOPTION-subscribe-request@listserv.gsa.gov with nothing in the subject.

Note: Federal government employees only are eligible to join.
